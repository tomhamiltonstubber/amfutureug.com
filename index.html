AmFuture is run by Ugandans, for Ugandans and all the money that goes to AmFuture goes directly to Ugandans. AmFuture is supported by a volunteer international board and a supporter base in Australia and the Netherlands. AmFuture and the programs it runs have grown organically around the visions and dreams of the young people involved.
